## Brief
A lightweight experiment tracker that keeps model runs, metrics, and artifacts aligned across teams.

## Highlights
- Created an experiment comparison view for model selection.
- Added artifact retention policies and lineage graphs.
- Implemented a model registry with approval workflows.

## Outcome
Improved reproducibility and cut onboarding time for new ML hires.
